Privacy

Your notes never
leave your phone.

Last updated · 16 June 2026

The short version

Recalla runs entirely on your device. There is no account, no cloud, and no server holding your data. We don't collect, sell, or even see your contacts or memories. The only time anything is sent off your phone is if you choose to use Recalla AI — and then only the note text you ask it to process.

01What Recalla stores

Everything you put into Recalla — people, memories, reminders, your own card — is saved in a private database on your device only. It is never uploaded to us or anyone else.

  • Contacts, roles, birthdays and locations you add
  • Memory notes and the categories they're filed under
  • Reminders and your shareable card details

Because it lives on your device, deleting the app removes your data. Use your phone's backup if you want to keep a copy.

02What we don't collect

We want to be precise here, because most apps aren't. Recalla has no analytics SDK, no advertising, no tracking, and no telemetry.

  • We don't ask you to create an account or sign in
  • We don't know who you are or who your contacts are
  • We don't track usage, location, or device identifiers

03The card you share

When you share your card, its details are encoded directly into the link — there is no server storing it. Anyone with the link can read what you put on the card, so share it the same way you'd hand someone a business card.

Opening someone else's card link shows it in your browser. Nothing is uploaded; the card data travels only inside the link itself.

04Recalla AI (optional)

Recalla AI is an optional paid feature. When — and only when — you use it, the text of the note you're processing is sent to Google's Gemini API to transcribe a voice note or generate a brief.

Your contact list is never sent — only the note text you ask AI to handle.

Google processes that text under its own terms and does not use it to train its models for API requests. If you never turn on Recalla AI, nothing ever leaves your device. See Google's API terms for details on their handling.

05Permissions

Recalla asks only for what a feature needs, when you use it:

  • Microphone — to record voice notes (Recalla AI only)
  • Notifications — to deliver the reminders you set
  • Contacts — only if you choose to import someone

You can decline any of these and still use the rest of the app.

06Payments

Recalla Pro and Recalla AI are handled by Google Play billing. We never see your card number or payment details — Google processes the transaction and tells the app only whether your purchase is active.

07Children

Recalla is not directed at children under 13, and because we collect no personal information, we hold no data about anyone — including children.

08Changes to this policy

If we change how Recalla handles data, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above. Because the app is on-device first, any meaningful change would arrive with an app update you choose to install.

09Contact

Questions about privacy, or anything else? Email plkt.studios@gmail.com and a human at PLKT Studio will reply.
